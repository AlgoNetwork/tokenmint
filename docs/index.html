What is TokenMint

TokenMint is the easiest and fastest way to generate your own ERC-20 or ERC-223 tokens on the Ethereum network.
No coding skills are required. It takes less than a minute.

Create tokens now

How it works

You complete our simple form

You fill up a simple form, specifying your account, token type, name, symbol, decimals, and initial supply. We will never ask you for your private key.

We deploy your smart contract

We create a smart contract on the Ethereum network using account you provided and top it up with tokens. You are always in control of your funds and tokens.
